EDUCATION

I began my educational journey at SDN Silih Asuh 3 Kota Cirebon, where I studied from 2010 to 2016. The school was not far from home, making it easy to enjoy my daily routines. During these years, I actively participated in extracurricular activities, especially traditional dance. Performing in front of audiences at school events helped me gain early confidence and a love for cultural arts.

After graduating from elementary school, I continued to SMPN 13 Kota Cirebon from 2017 to 2019. Junior high was an exciting phase where I made many new friends and joined both the Red Cross Youth (PMR) and traditional dance activities. These experiences taught me teamwork, empathy, and self-expression. One of the most memorable moments was during our farewell ceremony, where my class performed a dance together — it was fun and unforgettable.

Next, I attended SMKS Wahidin Kota Cirebon from 2020 to 2022. Since the school was farther from home, I was often dropped off by my parents. In high school, I joined the English Club, which improved my speaking skills and helped me feel more confident using English in everyday situations. It was a space where I could share ideas, learn new expressions, and make friends with similar interests.

Currently, I’m continuing my education at STMIK IKMI Kota Cirebon, majoring in Information Systems. I’m excited about this chapter of my life, where I get to explore the world of technology and grow academically and personally.
