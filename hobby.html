HOBBY

I have a hobby of reading books, and my love for reading has become an inseparable part of my life. My favorite book is the "Harry Potter Series" by J.K. Rowling. I've always been fascinated by the thrilling adventures of Harry, Hermione, and Ron in the series. Every time I read these books, I feel like I’m on an adventure at Hogwarts, and it’s such a special experience for me.

For me, books are not just a form of entertainment, but also a way to briefly escape from the real world. Reading allows me to explore endless worlds, meet interesting characters, and understand different perspectives. Besides that, it also helps me continue to learn and grow. I feel truly grateful for having this reading hobby.

So, for me, reading is a doorway to adventure, knowledge, and unlimited imagination. It’s an activity I enjoy every single day, and I never get tired of turning the pages of books that inspire me.
